About myself

Welcome to L.A. Massage & Wellness, your personal path to wellness. My name is Leigh Ann Withrow, and I am a certified massage therapist with a passion for promoting health and wellbeing through massage therapy.

I graduated from the esteemed massage therapy program at Sandhills Community College in Southern Pines in 2000.

I became a massage therapist because I wanted to make a real difference in people's lives. In a world that's increasingly stressful and fast-paced, I believe that taking care of our bodies and minds is not a luxuryâ€”it's a necessity. I was drawn to the therapeutic power of touch from a young age and inspired by its potential to both soothe and revitalize. Massage therapy gives me the opportunity to provide physical relief, promote healing, and help others find a sense of balance in their lives.

When I'm not providing massage therapy, I'm continually learning and exploring different wellness practices. I enjoy hiking in the great outdoors, practicing yoga, and studying holistic health approaches. These activities not only enrich my life, but they also deepen my understanding of well-being and inform my practice.

My dedication to your wellness goes beyond the massage table. With a tailored approach to each session, I combine technical knowledge with intuitive touch to work towards your specific goals. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, needing to unwind from the daily stresses, or aspiring to maintain a healthy lifestyle, I am committed to supporting you on your wellness journey.
