Welcome to L.A. Massage & Wellness, your personal path to wellness. My name is Leigh Ann Withrow, and I am a certified massage therapist with a passion for promoting health and wellbeing through massage therapy.

I graduated from the esteemed massage therapy program at Sandhills Community College in Southern Pines in 2020.

As a mother of four, including twins, I understand the physical challenges that can come with motherhood. When I experienced back pain, I relied on various methods for relief, including lidocaine and massage. Through my own journey, I discovered that massage is the healthiest form of recovery, providing not only physical relief but also emotional and psychological benefits.

I'm also an avid runner, and I've experienced firsthand how massage can play a crucial role in injury prevention and recovery. Physical touch is a fundamental human need, and even a regular Swedish massage can have long-term positive effects on our overall well-being.

My dedication to your wellness goes beyond the massage table. With a tailored approach to each session, I combine technical knowledge with intuitive touch to work towards your specific goals. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, needing to unwind from the daily stresses, or aspiring to maintain a healthy lifestyle, I am committed to supporting you on your wellness journey.
